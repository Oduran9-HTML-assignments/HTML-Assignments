Colombian empanadas

Ingredients Instructions
  • 2 cups pre-cooked yellow cornmeal (masarepa)
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 1/2 cup cooked and shredded beef
  • 1/2 cup cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup cooked and diced potatoes
  • 1/2 cup cooked and diced onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  1. In a large bowl, combine the pre-cooked yellow cornmeal (masarepa) with warm water. Knead until you get a soft, pliable dough.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the shredded beef, shredded chicken, diced potatoes, diced onions, chopped green onions, chopped hard-boiled eggs, cilantro, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Take a small portion of the dough, flatten it in your hand, and place a spoonful of the filling in the center. Fold the dough over the filling, shaping it into a half-moon. Seal the edges to form the empanada.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C). Fry the empanadas until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Remove from the oil and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious Colombian empanadas!
